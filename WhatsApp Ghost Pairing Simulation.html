How the Hack Works

  • Social Engineering: The attacker uses trust (a "friend") to bypass suspicion.
  • The "Ghost" Step: The attacker goes to WhatsApp Web, enters the victim's phone number, and clicks "Link with phone number."
  • The Intercept: WhatsApp generates an 8-digit code. The attacker displays a fake box to the victim to "verify identity."
  • Takeover: Once the victim enters that code into the fake site, the attacker's browser is now Linked to the victim's WhatsApp.

How to Stay Safe

1

Never share pairing codes. WhatsApp codes are for linking *your* own devices only.

2

Enable 2FA. Go to Settings > Account > Two-step verification.

3

Verify Links. Hover over links. Facebook links will never end in .net or .archive.

4

Voice Check. If a friend sends a weird link, call them to confirm it's actually them.
